About This Package

The Delhi to Shimla road trip is the quintessential North India hill station escape — a scenic drive through Haryana's plains, through the Shivalik foothills and up the legendary Kalka-Shimla highway to the Queen of Hills. With your personal driver handling all the mountain roads, you simply sit back and absorb the colonial charm, cedar forests, and sweeping Himalayan views.

Shimla, the former summer capital of British India, offers a unique blend of Victorian architecture, vibrant Mall Road bazaars, and thrilling viewpoints like Jakhu Hill and Kufri. Our drivers have perfected this route and know every scenic stop and local eatery along the way.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

01
Day 1
Delhi — Chandigarh — Shimla
Depart Delhi early morning via NH44 towards Chandigarh (250 km). After a breakfast halt in Chandigarh, continue on the scenic Kalka-Shimla highway — winding roads through dense forests with the Ghaggar river valley below. Arrive Shimla by afternoon. Check in and explore the famous Mall Road and The Ridge in the evening.
Delhi Chandigarh Shimla
02
Day 2
Shimla Sightseeing — Jakhu Temple, Kufri, Christ Church
Full day local sightseeing in your car. Start with Jakhu Temple — the highest point in Shimla with panoramic Himalayan views and resident langurs. Drive to Kufri for snow activities and adventure sports. Return to explore Christ Church, the iconic Gaiety Theatre, and Scandal Point on Mall Road. Evening stroll on The Ridge.
Jakhu Temple Kufri Mall Road
03
Day 3
Shimla — Delhi (Return)
Morning at leisure. Optional visit to Chail (the world's highest cricket ground) or Naldehra golf course before departure. Drive back to Delhi via the same scenic Kalka highway, arriving by early evening. Drop-off at your preferred Delhi location.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Shimla?
October to February for snowfall and a magical winter experience, March to June for pleasant weather ideal for sightseeing, and September for lush green post-monsoon views. Summers (April–June) are most popular with families escaping Delhi's heat.
How long does the drive from Delhi to Shimla take?
The drive typically takes 7–8 hours covering approximately 345 km. This includes a breakfast break in Chandigarh and time to enjoy the scenic mountain section from Kalka to Shimla. We recommend an early morning departure from Delhi.
Is the price per person or per car?
All our prices are per car, not per person. Whether you're 2 or 6 people in the car, the price remains the same — making it very economical for groups and families. Split the cost and save more!
Can we visit Kufri and Chail on the same trip?
Yes, absolutely. Kufri is 13 km from Shimla and Chail is about 45 km. Both can be covered comfortably in a day trip from Shimla. Just let us know your preferences while booking and we'll plan the day accordingly.
Can we customize the itinerary to add Manali or Kasauli?
100%. You can add Kasauli, Naldehra, Narkanda, or even extend to Manali. Just let us know while booking and we'll plan the extended itinerary with adjusted pricing. All our packages are fully customizable.
