Car With Driver North India ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website at carwithdrivernorthindia.com or contact us for our services.
1
Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Fill in our contact or booking enquiry form
  • Call or WhatsApp us directly
  • Send us an email

This information may include your name, phone number, email address, travel dates, pickup/drop locations, and passenger details.

2
How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the purpose of:

  • Responding to your booking enquiry or quote request
  • Confirming and managing your travel booking
  • Contacting you regarding your journey (driver details, updates)
  • Improving our services based on feedback

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3
Data Storage

Enquiry data submitted through our website forms is stored securely in Google Sheets, accessible only to our team. We retain customer data for a reasonable period to manage booking history and service quality. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4
Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to enhance browsing experience (e.g. remembering form entries). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our site.

5
Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms (WhatsApp, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

6
Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for future communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@carwithdrivernorthindia.com.

8
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
